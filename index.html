DANISH DESIGN FURNITURE BY INTERIOR

INTERIOR was born in Denmark in 1984 and is today a premium retail lifestyle brand. We design, produce, and sell a range of contemporary Danish design furniture, accessories, and lighting for the living room, dining room, bedroom, home-office, and outdoor spaces. Our furniture stores offer everything within modern design ranging from designer sofas and armchairs to practical wall units and functional TV units. We also help our customers create a personal home with our comprehensive in home or store Interior Design service . Interior decorators incorporate interior decoration ideas, home accessories, and more to craft a unique style for your home. Our interior decorators can help you in creating a Nordic mood in the dining room with Scandinavian style sideboards, elegant dining tables, and dining chairs - or a hotel vibe in your living room with sofas, recliner armchairs, and daybeds in leather, velvet, or your preferred fabric. The highly skilled interior decorators are available in all our furniture stores where they will advise you in store, or come to your home, listen to your needs, and visualize different solutions to help you make your dream home come true.

Come visit us in store and see our wide range of multifunctional design furniture and get professional advice about modern living and interior design. The furniture stores showcase our Scandinavian furniture range and inspires you with the latest Nordic style and design. The store staff and interior designers help you find the perfect style to match your personality and your home decor. When browsing through our designer furniture, there is a whole line of unseen possibilities behind everything you see. Our furniture can be personalized exactly to your needs and taste in a spectrum of beautiful colors and materials perfectly coordinated so your look is consistently carried through in your home. Aniline leather, felt, velvet, oak, walnut, chrome, and steel - We combine materials and surfaces in beautiful custom compositions that highlight creations from Danish designers and world famous international designers to create personal design expressions. We collaborate with designers to promote our Danish design history and combine it with modern and contemporary trends and technology. Visit us in our stores today to see Danish design excellence rooted in heritage with a keen eye to the future..
